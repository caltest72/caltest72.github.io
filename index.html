Some tips to get you started:

Watch the numbers in your meetings.

Think about what you want to achieve from this meeting.

If you need to make a decision or commit to an action, there should be no more than 8 people in the room. Any more than this your job gets harder.

For brainstorming or planning sessions you can go a bit higher, but stick to a maximum of 20. Any more and people will hide away.

Feedback is Key.

Everyone has been in a meeting they'd rather not be in, minds wandering, eyes glazing, perhaps you have even run one.

It is not something to hide away from. As with everything, the only way we can improve is Feedback!

So spark this change - ask your participants to give you constructive feedback. It could be, that a simple change engages even more minds.
